About

I am a doctoral researcher at the Florida Atlantic University-Boca Raton under the supervision of Prof. Zhen Ni in the MiS Lab. My research lives at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Healthcare, where I’m driven by the challenge of bridging human clinical expertise with machine intelligence.

Specifically, I focus on Federated Learning, exploring how we can build clinical decision support systems in decentralized environments. My goal is to ensure these systems aren't just powerful, but also personalized, fair, and reliable enough to be trusted in real-world medical settings.

I bring strong expertise in computer vision, data science methodologies, federated learning, machine learning, and deep learning, with reviews in top journals and conferences.

Beyond the lab, I am deeply committed to the academic community. As a Teaching Assistant, I have the privilege of mentoring the next generation of engineers and scientists, helping them navigate the complexities of machine learning and data science/analytics.
